# **App Name**: MediSite

## Core Features:

- Homepage: Display essential hospital information including emergency contact, OPD timings, key services, and navigation.
- Department Directory: Provide a comprehensive directory of departments with detailed information on services, treatments, and available doctors.
- Doctor Search and Profiles: Enable users to search for doctors by name or department, and view detailed profiles including expertise and OPD timings.
- Appointment Booking: Allow patients to book appointments with specific doctors or departments, with options for in-person or telemedicine consultations.
- Health Package Recommendations: AI-powered tool to recommend relevant health checkup packages to the user, tailored to specific needs based on their history and profile.
- Emergency Services Locator: Immediate access to emergency contact numbers, ambulance booking, and hospital location for quick assistance.
- Contact Form: Enable users to send their query through a contact form and receive immediate support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calming blue (#64B5F6), promoting trust and tranquility.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#F0F4F8), offering a clean, professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft teal (#4DB6AC) for call-to-action buttons and important links.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a clean, modern, and easily readable interface.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) to ensure readability and comfort in longer text sections.
- Use flat, minimalist icons in blue and teal to represent departments, services, and key features.
- A clean, responsive layout with clear sections for easy navigation. Prioritize mobile-first design for accessibility on all devices.
- Subtle animations, such as smooth scrolling and hover effects on clickable elements, to enhance user experience without being distracting.
